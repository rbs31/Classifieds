📋 About Classifieds

Classifieds is a free, open-source classified ads platform designed for North America's diverse, multi-ethnic communities. Whether you're looking for housing, a job, services, or selling used items — Classifieds connects you in your own language.

🎯 Our Mission

Immigrant and ethnic communities across North America often struggle with language barriers when using traditional classified platforms. Classifieds solves this by automatically detecting the language of each listing and showing users content in languages they understand — all on the same platform.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is Classifieds free to use?

A: Yes! Classifieds is completely free for both posting and browsing ads. No fees, no subscriptions.

Q: Which languages does Classifieds support?

A: Classifieds supports 12 languages: English, Chinese (中文), Spanish (Español), Korean (한국어), Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt), Filipino, Hindi (हिन्दी), Japanese (日本語), French (Français), Portuguese, Russian, and Arabic. The platform automatically detects the language of each listing.

Q: How does the language detection work?

A: When you post a listing, Classifieds analyzes the text to detect which languages are used. When browsing, you can filter to see only listings in languages you understand. This helps ethnic communities find relevant ads while staying connected to the broader market.

Q: Which cities does Classifieds cover?

A: Currently: New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Toronto, Vancouver, Chicago, Houston, Seattle, Boston, and Miami. We're expanding to more cities based on demand.

Q: How is Classifieds different from Craigslist?

A: While Craigslist is English-centric, Classifieds is built from the ground up for multi-ethnic communities. Listings are automatically tagged by language, and the interface adapts to show you content in languages you understand. It's like having separate Craigslist instances for each language community, but all on one platform.

Q: Can I post in multiple languages?

A: Yes! You can post a listing with titles/descriptions in multiple languages (e.g., English + Chinese). Classifieds will detect both languages and show your listing to users browsing in either language.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Classifieds is open-source. You can review the code on GitHub. We don't sell your data or show invasive ads.

Q: How often is the data updated?

A: Listing data is automatically refreshed daily at 1:00 AM (Pacific Time) to ensure fresh, relevant results.
